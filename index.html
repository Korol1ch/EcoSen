Ecology of the Future: A Step Toward a Clean Planet

We are –
a team of proactive and creative young people who believe in the power of small steps and big ideas. We are united by a desire to make our city more modern and eco-friendly. INFINITY is a place where everyone matters: every participant contributes, shares ideas, and grows together with the team. We move forward and turn ideas into reality.
Our mission –
is to inspire people to care about the environment and make eco-friendly behavior a natural part of everyone’s life. We aim to show that the small steps of each person lead to big results: cleaner streets, a healthier environment, and a responsible attitude toward nature. INFINITY strives to unite those who are ready to act, support eco-initiatives, and create a city people want to live in.
Our goal –
is to make caring for the environment interesting and accessible to everyone. We want people to easily help nature: recycle waste, receive coins for it, and exchange them for prizes. This way, we turn eco-friendly actions into a fun and useful process where both people and the planet benefit.
